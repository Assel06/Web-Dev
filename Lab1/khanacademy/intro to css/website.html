Travel to Lake Como

Top 3 places to visit:

  1. Bellagio

    2. Known as the "Pearl of Lake Como," Bellagio is famous for its picturesque streets, elegant villas, and beautiful gardens. Visit Villa Melzi and enjoy the views from Punta Spartivento.

  2. Varenna

    3. A charming village with narrow streets, Varenna is home to the Villa Monastero and the medieval Castle of Vezio. Don't miss a stroll along the lake promenade.

  3. Menaggio

    4. Menaggio is a lovely town with a scenic lakefront, a historic center, and a prominent golf club. Take a boat ride to explore nearby attractions.
